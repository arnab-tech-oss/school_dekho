List of Best CBSE schools, ICSE schools in DELHI, Best schools in DELHI

  • 12 Dec-2022

  • Approx Read Time: 4 Minutes

  • Posted By: School Dekho Content Team

Delhi is a metropolis and the national capital of central India. The historic city of Old Delhi is located in the north of the city, and New Delhi is located in the south. This blog is all about helping parents to choose the best cbse and icse schools in Delhi.  The Government of Delhi's Education Department is a leading agency that handles educational matters. According to the RTE right to education, all children between the ages of six and fourteen must get an education. Additionally, all private schools reserve 25 percent of their seats for children from low-income families.

 

The state of Uttar Pradesh borders the area to the east, while the states of Haryana are to the north, west, and south.

 

There are many famous schools in Delhi, here all the schools names with information given below those are very famous. The admissions process for nursery school will begin on December 1st, 2022.In this blog post, we delve into the top schools in Delhi, showcasing their academic prowess, infrastructure, co-curricular activities, and overall reputation. These institutions have consistently demonstrated excellence in shaping young minds and preparing students for a bright future.

 

The top schools in Delhi for the academic year 2022,2023 are included on this list.

Best Schools in Delhi:-

 

1- Sanskriti school:-

 

It is affiliated with CBSE and it's located at - Dr. S. Radhakrishnan Marg Chanakyapuri, New Delhi- 110021.

 

2- Vasant Valley School:-

 

It started in 1990, is CBSE affiliated, and is located at Vasant Kunj Rd, Pocket 7, Sector C, Vasant Kunj, New Delhi-110070.

 

3- The Shri Ram School:- 

 

It is a day school and is affiliated with ICSE & CISCE. Located at D3 St, Block D, Vasant Vihar, New Delhi, Delhi 110057.

 

4- Mother’s International School:- 

 

It is a day school and is affiliated with CBSE. Situated in Sri Aurobindo Marg, Vijay Mandal Enclave, Kalu Sarai, New Delhi- 110016.

 

5- Sardar Patel Vidyalaya:- 

 

It is a day school and is affiliated with CBSE. Located at  Sardar Patel Vidyalaya, Lodhi Estate, New Delhi, Delhi 110003.

 

6- Springdales School:- 

 

It was established in 1955 and is affiliated with CBSE. It is an co-ed school and is situated at Springdales School Pusa Road, Upper Ridge Road Junction, New Delhi -110005.

 

7- Delhi Public School:- 

 

It was founded in 1972 and is a CBSE-affiliated school. Located at  Sector-XII, R K Puram. New Delhi 110022. 

 

8- Modern School:- 

 

This school was founded in 1920, a century ago. It is co-ed school and is affiliated to withSE. Located at Barakhamba Rd, Todermal Road Area, Mandi House, New Delhi 110001.

 

9- Nirmal Bhartiya Public School:- 

 

It is a co-ed and CBSE-affiliated school. Located at Sector 14, Dwarka, New Delhi 110078.

 

10- Loreto Convent School:- 

 

This school was founded in 1964 and is a CBSE-affiliated school. Situated in Delhi Cantt, New Delhi.
